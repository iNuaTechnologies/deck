Primary Barrier: Usage Costs

The majority of Africa's population lives in rural zones, yet nearly three-quarters of this specific demographic remain disconnected.

Critical Bottleneck

This disconnect represents a significant limitation regarding information accessibility and economic distribution.

The Problem

A Vital Disconnect

Internet is a revolution in knowledge sharing and scaling solutions for both clients and customers.
Connectivity is not a luxury but fundamental for growth.
An undeniable amount of organizations has no access to use advance technologies to scale their work.
However, infrastructure and costs create a massive barrier for rural populations.

The Solution

Instead of waiting for these barriers to be resolved in order to use technology, we use technology to allow communication in scale... despite it.

How It Works

Long Term Vision

The "SMS Search Engine"

By connecting thousands of organizations, iNua creates a centralized database of vital services.

In the long term, this allows us to offer a reverse-lookup service. Users can query needs like "Water aid", "Vaccinations", or "Market prices", and our system identifies the relevant organization from our network to provide the answer.
